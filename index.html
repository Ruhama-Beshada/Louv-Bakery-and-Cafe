About Us

Welcome to Louv Bakery & Café, your neighborhood spot for handcrafted cakes and comforting beverages. We specialize in custom-designed cakes for birthdays, weddings, and special occasions. We also serve a variety of hot and cold drinks in a cozy space — perfect for relaxing, catching up with friends, or celebrating sweet moments. Every item at Louv is made with high-quality ingredients and a touch of love.
